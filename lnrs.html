Background and responsibilities

I lead the UX function at Lexis Nexis working across multiple web based products and mobile applications. I work with teams who build digital identity, tracing, risk and compliance products – sold primarily in the financial services, insurance and pensions sectors.

Being the first UX hire within the UK company, I plan and conduct research activities across our product portfolio, design prototypes to test with users as well as shaping the products from a visual design, usability and accessibility perspective.
