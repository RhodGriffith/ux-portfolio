Our brand

Our primary palette is comprised of red, black and white.

The LexisNexis® Risk Solutions brand is unique in that we were born out of a masterbrand, and continue to share that with our sister company LexisNexis® Legal and Professional. But we are very different from Legal and Professional, so over the past few years we’ve worked to refresh, redefine and customize the Risk Solutions brand in order to clarify who we are and avoid any confusion for our customers.

The LexisNexis® Risk Solutions logo

The LexisNexis Risk Solutions logo is the preferred logo for the company. It should be used in almost all cases, with very few exceptions.

Position, size, color and spatial proportions of the LexisNexis Risk Solutions logo elements must never be altered.

The registered trademark symbols (®) for both the Knowledge Burst and the logotype must always be present to protect the identity of the LexisNexis Masterbrand. The registered trademark symbol should always be placed to the right of both the Knowledge Burst and the logotype as shown.

Selective use of the Knowledge Burst without the logotype must be approved by Brand Marketing and the RELX IP Group. Selective use may apply to applications such as mobile, social media and video.

Product usage

The product name will no longer be visually presented “locked up” with the LexisNexis master logo. The Risk Solutions logo should be added, the pipe eliminated and the appropriate clear space added be- tween the LNRS logo and the product name.

*Clear space between logo and product name should be the size of the knowledge burst
